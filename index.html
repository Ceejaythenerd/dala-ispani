CV Cheat Sheet

Quick tips to get your CV noticed.

1. The Golden Rule

Keep it to 2 pages maximum. Recruiters spend about 6 seconds scanning a CV. Make it count.

2. Contact Info

Use a professional email (e.g., name.surname@gmail.com). Do not use "sexyboy99@yahoo.com".

3. No Experience?

List your soft skills (Leadership, Teamwork, Communication) and any volunteer work or school projects.

4. File Format

Always save as PDF unless asked otherwise. Word docs can look messy on different phones.
